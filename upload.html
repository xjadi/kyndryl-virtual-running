📋 Tips for Best Results

📱
Full Screen Screenshot

Capture the full results screen after completing your run. Make sure all stats are visible and the screen isn't partially cut off.

💡
Good Lighting & Resolution

For photos of physical devices, ensure good lighting with no glare. Screenshots from apps tend to work best.

🏷️
Show Your Name

Make sure your profile name is visible in the screenshot. You can always edit it in the form before submitting.

🔍
Always Review First

After AI extraction, review all fields before submitting. You can edit any values the AI got wrong. Name and Distance are required.
